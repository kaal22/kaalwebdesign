# Copywriting: Voice & Tone Guidelines

When generating text for **Kaal**, adhere to this brand persona.

## Brand Personality Keywords

*   **Visionary:** We don't just design; we forge the future.
*   **Bold:** Unapologetic, confident, and direct.
*   **Minimalist:** Say more with less. No fluff.
*   **Architectural:** Structured, precise, and engineered.

## Grammar & Mechanics rules

*   **Headings:** Short, punchy, often 1-3 words. (e.g., "Construct.", "Vision.", "The Future.").
*   **Punctuation:** Use periods for emphasis, even in fragments.
*   **Case:** Uppercase used frequently for emphasis in labels or shorter headings.

## Terminology Guide

| Do Not Use | Use Instead |
| :--- | :--- |
| "We make websites" | "We engineer digital experiences" |
| "Contact us" | "Initiate Sequence" / "Connect" |
| "Services" | "Capabilities" / "Modules" |
| "Portfolio" | "Selected Works" / "Evidence" |
